Windshield Repair in Corinth, MS

We provide a variety of windshield repair services in Corinth and MS, including chip and crack repair, water leak protection, and reinstallation of loose molding. The most frequently requested service is chip repair. The purpose of repairing chips that are still small is to prevent them from cracking. Cracks can be hazardous.

It is critical to repair chipped and cracked surfaces immediately. Once a chip becomes a crack, it cannot be repaired. The windshield must be replaced in its entirety. Leave unattended chips to absorb water and other debris. Water absorbed by the chip can then freeze, resulting in extensive irreparable cracks. Debris absorbed by the chip can cause the glass to delaminate, making it opaque and difficult to see through.
